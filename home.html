The Plan

My current plan is to first work in the finance world before ultimately starting my own fashion company. I plan to use my internship experience in investments to pursue a career in venture capital or financial consulting for a few years (while also founding a streetwear startup brand on the side), before going to business school to earn an MBA (preferably in fashion and luxury management) and eventually founding my own fashion startup that focuses on sustainable and ethically-sourced materials.

Why?

There are 2 main reasons that I want to be involved in the fashion industry. The first is my love for fashion. I have always been heavily interested in style, and would love to utilize my business skills in the fashion world. The fashion industry is one that we all to some extent—whether we like it or not—interact with. Interestingly enough, clothing is something of a necessity in our present culture. And there are so many iterations, variations, and extremes that can be found within the fashion industry that I believe fashion is a perfectly dynamic and innovative industry for me to immerse myself in. The second is my desire to redefine men’s fashion. Even today, most clothing options for men are comparatively basic and repetitive. More interesting clothing options for men are usually physically or financially inaccessible, or are not widely known to the public.

Why A Website?

I believe that creating a website to present this project serves several important utilities. First, I believe that this is an excellent opportunity to further practice and improve my coding and web design skills. In addition, I believe that creating a website is a unique way of visualizing my future plans, and adds a layer of interactiveness and immersion with the content. Lastly, I believe that maintaining a website is not only crucial for a successful fashion company, but also incredibly important in beginning to establish a personal and professional brand.
